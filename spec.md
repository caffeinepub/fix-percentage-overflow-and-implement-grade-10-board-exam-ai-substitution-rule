# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Enhance Subject Analysis with cross-term and cross-grade subject averages, and fix Computer Science max marks configuration for Grades 1–8.

**Planned changes:**
- In Subject Analysis, calculate and display the average percentage of each subject across all terms within each grade, clearly labeled as a per-grade average.
- In Subject Analysis, add a summary section showing each subject's overall average percentage across all grades and all terms.
- Update the Computer Science max marks so that Grades 1–7 use 20 marks and Grade 8 uses 30 marks, reflected in all percentage calculations and the marks entry form.

**User-visible outcome:** Users can see how each subject performs on average across all terms of a grade, as well as a full cross-grade subject average in Subject Analysis. Computer Science percentages are now calculated correctly based on the grade-specific total marks (20 for Grades 1–7, 30 for Grade 8).
